Industry data shows that 63% of first-level insurance appeals result in partial or full payment. The overturn rate varies by CARC code and documentation quality. Does AI guarantee my denial will be overturned?

No tool guarantees an overturn. Denial Appeal Pro improves your odds by generating a CARC-specific appeal letter with the correct terminology, documentation checklist, and rebuttal structure that payers expect. Which denials are hardest to beat?

Denials with no supporting documentation — such as services with no operative notes or claims filed genuinely outside the timely filing window — have the lowest overturn rates. Denials caused by missing information, incorrect coding, or payer processing errors have the highest overturn rates when appealed with the right records attached.

File your first-level appeal as soon as possible. Most payers allow 30 to 180 days from the denial date. Denials appealed within the first 30 days have slightly higher overturn rates because records are fresh and deadlines are not at risk.
