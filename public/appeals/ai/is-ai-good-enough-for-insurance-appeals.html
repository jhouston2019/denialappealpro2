Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI appeal letter quality compare to a billing specialist?

AI-generated appeal letters match billing specialist quality on structure, CARC terminology, and rebuttal logic. Denial Appeal Pro produces letters with the same sections a specialist would include — claim identification, denial restatement, targeted rebuttal, and attachment checklist. Can payers tell if an appeal letter was written by AI?

Payers evaluate appeals based on the argument and supporting documentation, not authorship. There is no requirement to disclose that a letter was AI-generated. The letter either addresses the denial reason with adequate evidence or it does not. AI letters use standard medical billing terminology that payers process daily.

What are the limits of AI appeal letter quality?

AI works from the information on your denial letter and its training on billing standards. It cannot verify clinical facts in your medical records or know payer-specific quirks not reflected in the denial. Complex cases involving multiple CARC codes, litigation, or regulatory disputes may need specialist review beyond AI output. Always review before submission.

Should I trust AI with high-dollar denials?

AI generates a strong first draft for any denial amount. For high-dollar claims above 10,000 dollars, add a specialist review step to verify clinical references and payer-specific requirements. Denial Appeal Pro saves the 45-minute writing step regardless of claim value — the review depth scales with the dollar amount at risk.
