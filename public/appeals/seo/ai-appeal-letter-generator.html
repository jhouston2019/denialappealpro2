The Manual Appeal Bottleneck

Every denied claim in your queue needs a unique appeal letter referencing the specific CARC code, payer policy, and clinical or billing evidence. With denial rates averaging 10 to 15% of submitted claims, mid-size practices accumulate 50 to 200 appealable denials per month. Manual writing cannot keep pace without adding headcount.
